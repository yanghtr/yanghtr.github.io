Haitao Yang

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Qixing Huang. I received my Bachelor degree with honors in 2019 from Department of Electrical Engineering, Tsinghua University. During my undergraduate studies, I was advised by Prof. Yebin Liu.

My research focuses on 3D vision, computer graphics and machine learning.
